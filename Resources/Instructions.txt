NIN-JUH
HOW TO PLAY:


AIM OF THE GAME:
The main objective of the game is to correctly spell the name of the fruit that appears on the screen.

INSTRUCTIONS:
1.	Look at the fruit appearing on the screen. 

2.	Look at the fruit name; notice that there is a missing letter.
 
3.	Using the box, correctly fill in the fruit name using the textbox to correspond with the fruit showing on the screen. 

4.	If you correctly place the right letter/s to complete the fruit name, your current health you will remain the same.

5.	If you incorrectly place the wrong letter, your current health will decrease.

6.	Remember to enjoy the game!
